Meet Katherine

Fitness has always been a part of Katherine’s life, starting from an early age. She started swimming before she could barely walk and had her first gym membership by the time she was 11 years old. She played many sports growing up, but found a true love in dance. Despite being always physically active, Katherine struggled with weight through high school. She found the secret to a healthy weight and body image is through a healthy lifestyle that consists of balance.

Over 10 years of ballet, jazz and various styles of dance training, and 8 years of yoga practice, has helped Katherine refine body movement, flow and form. Having recovered from many injuries herself, she understands the importance of proper alignment and building full body strength from the largest muscles to the tiniest. She has experience working with a diverse array of clients to help them reach their goals, from losing weight to improving athletic ability.

She focuses on pushing the limits of the human body, while maintaining proper form and execution of the exercises. She believes that your attitude in the gym and the effort your put into your workouts will transfer over into how you live your life. Katherine uses her passions for fitness to inspire and motivate her clients and to help them transform their bodies, as well as their overall well-being.

Katherine has a tendency to take on everything she does with full force; she picked up running a few years ago and hasn’t stopped since. She finds sanity in this crazy city through distance running. She challenges anyone who says they “hate running,” or “can’t run,” to give her a chance to transform their thinking.

Services

Katherine utilizes a dynamic integrated training style that will always keep you on your toes (no pun intended!). She believes physical fitness should be challenging and FUN. Her mission is not only to help her clients improve their fitness level, but also to educate them, so that the changes they make together can be maintained throughout a lifetime. Katherine will work with you to set goals together during the initial session, which is free of charge.

Katherine trains throughout the NYC area. Private training & small group training packages are available for 30, 45, 60 and 75-minute sessions that can be arranged in your home, office, gym, or one of the studio’s that Katherine partners with in Flatiron, or the Upper East Side. Programs are entirely customized, flexible and ongoing with the clients’ goals at the forefront.
